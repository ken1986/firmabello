International Shipping

FirmaBello BE is one of Germany's first class shipping service provider, offering reliable service at a competitive rate. Whether you're looking for container shipment, individual baggage shipment, overseas removals or corporate relocation, you can be sure that you'll receive the best service and that your belongings and goods will be delivered securely, safely, and on time.

Our extensive network goes beyond Germany and Europe, to cater to the global logistics needs of customers shipping products to Africa, USA, North America, and Asia. We are specialists in logistics services and have the uncanny reputation for being very effective, organized, and timely, with friendly, professional, and honest staff. We ensure that your shipping experience becomes unrivaled by providing constant communication. Our online tracking service allows you to monitor the progress of your product - car, containers, and personal belongings - in its journey to its final destination.

As a registered and licensed company, we pride ourselves on providing the best protection to our clients' goods, making sure that quality shipping standards are implemented. Our men will go out of their way to offer quality service. Our administrative team makes sure that all the shipping documentation is processed on time, and that your goods arrive in pristine condition

Project Financing

FirmaBello BE offers project financing services and quality project implementation coordination for project owners, organizations, governments, private and public companies. The financing services are designed to offer support mostly to projects with social backgrounds such as housing, drinking water supply, education, health services, energy, and infrastructure. Our scope of financing services is wide and also includes commercial and business projects.

Project funding volume/need of USD or Euro 100 -300M Liquid Assets worth a minimum of 100M Euros or 100M USD, which could be cash funds or cash backed bank instrument 30% Equity from Project Owner is required as an alternative in some cases

Some of the projects that attract the interest of FirmaBello BE Ltd will include infrastructure projects such as highways, railroads, sea ports, and airports; housing projects such as resorts and hotels; projects that are related to tourism, alternative and solar energy projects, and those that are designed to offer the best amenities to the public such as hospitals, schools, universities, and professional training centers, and many others.

Any company or project owner seeking our financing services should first send us a comprehensive executive copy of the project via email. We will get back to the client within few days of receipt of project description with more details if we are interested.
